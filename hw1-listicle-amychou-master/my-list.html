Abhinav's Cafe: 3 Amazing beverages you should be drinking
Abhinav Puri
Last updated Apr 13, 2021
1.
Oreo Milkshake
Near Block A, CCET(DG), Sector 26

When's the last time you've had an amazing Oreo Milkshake? It had been a while for me, but I wanted to join my friend for a non-caffeinated beverage at Bytes and I became reaquainted with the combination of thick milk and oreo. Oreo Milkshake are not so sweet as a normal drinks and not so boring as a others flavored carbonated water. The Oreo Milkshake at Bytes also have cream in them , which makes it a nice drink alternative for the times when you're feeling a refreshing drink that doesn't have caffeine.

Recommendation: Oreo Milkshake + Fries

2.
Lemonade
Near Block A, CCET(DG), Sector 26

Lemonade is a sweetened lemon-flavored beverage. There are varieties of lemonade found throughout the world. In North America and South Asia, cloudy lemonade dominates. It is traditionally a homemade drink using lemon juice, water, and a sweetener such as cane sugar, simple syrup or honey. They have little bites to eat too, like popcorn chicken and eggrolls, in perfect snack-size portions. There's not too much seating, but there's a cute outdoor area in the back for warmer days.

Lemonade isn't exactly on campus and it's a short walk from there. (5 minutes total from the Gates Computer Science Building!)

Recommendation: Lemonade + sandwich.

3.
Cold Coffee
Near Block A, CCET(DG), Sector 26

I can't tell you whether cold coffee has a particularly amazing drink, only because cold coffee is the only drink I've ever ordered as regular basis. I'm not even totally sure I know what a cold coffee is supposed to taste like. What I do know is the cold coffee I ordered on a whim from here was delicious.

Recommendation: Cold Coffee
